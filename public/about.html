About us

Welcome! While you're here we might as well tell you about the background of this website.

It began in the Fall term of 2020. As a final project we had to create a website to show off our web development skills. An idea immediately came to us. We had heard from friends that there was constant frustration with the way tutoring was handled at Oregon State Universtiy and we knew we could solve that problem. Simply put, we are a group of 4 Oregon State University students who shared the same goal of creating an easily accessible website for students to use to find tutors.

We'd like to appreciate not only the time built creating this website as a team but the marvelous professor Hess for bringing us on this journey.
